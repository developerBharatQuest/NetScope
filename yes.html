Why NetScope?

Understanding your network status shouldn't require a degree in computer science. We created this tool to give you a clear, visual snapshot of how the internet sees you. Whether you are troubleshooting connectivity, verifying your VPN location, or just curious, we provide the data you need in a layout that just works.

Instant Lookup

Bypass the command line. We query global geolocation APIs to pull your public network identity in milliseconds.

Visual Geolocation

Context matters. We render your approximate coordinates on an interactive map to help you visualize server regions and latency zones.

Universal Access

Built for speed and compatibility, our interface adapts seamlessly across your workstation, tablet, and mobile devices.
